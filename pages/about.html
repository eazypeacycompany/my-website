About Zakary

The Story and the Family

Born in 2004, I am the oldest of triplets in a family of eight with six children. Currently, I am one of four siblings attending Brigham Young University.

I have one older brother who is about to have a child, so I will soon become an uncle. My sister graduated from BYU with a degree in Publishing and Editing. Two other siblings are currently pursuing their own college and career goals.

My youngest sibling is autistic and lives in a group home. He is high-functioning and doing well. Beyond my family, this page focuses on my own experiences and journey.

Utah: 2004–2019

I grew up in Mountain Green, Utah, where I enjoyed the outdoors and a strong community.

My family and I helped remove invasive foliage, reducing fire risk and earning recognition for the city. We also participated in community service projects, helped elderly residents, and organized local events.

Through Ski Utah, I assisted with promotional media and had opportunities to meet several Olympians. Toward the end of middle school, my family moved to San Diego, California.

California: 2019–2022

In San Diego, I completed high school and earned my Eagle Scout rank by organizing an eyeglass drive with the San Diego Lions Club, collecting approximately 400 pairs of glasses.

I also worked as a lifeguard for the YMCA of San Diego and learned lifesaving skills. My family contributed to promotional media projects for Waterford Upstart, an early childhood education program.

Along with my high school diploma, I earned the ANA Numismatic Diploma, allowing me to evaluate coin condition and value.

Mission: 2022–2024

From November 2022 to November 2024, I served as a missionary in the Michigan Detroit Mission.

During this time, I served communities throughout Michigan and Ohio, worked with people from many backgrounds, and gained valuable leadership, communication, and service experience.

My mission helped me grow personally and strengthened my ability to work with individuals from all walks of life.

Current Life and Future Goals

I am currently attending Brigham Young University and pursuing a degree in Global Supply Chain Management.

I am also working on developing a business that I hope will make people's lives easier.
